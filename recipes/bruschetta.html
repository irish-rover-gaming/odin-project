Bruschetta Recipe

Home

Ingredients:
  • Bread Loaf
    • - French Bread is best suited for it's density and structure for toasting and soaking juices.
  • Extra-Vigin Olive Oil (EVOO) - 1 Tbls
  • Roma Tomatoes - 8
    • - Best Roma Tomatoes are very firm (not unripe).
  • Fresh Basil - 1⁄3Cup - chopped
  • Parmesean Cheese - 1 oz - grated
  • Optional: Fresh Mozzarella Cheese
  • Garlic - 2 cloves - minced
  • Balsamic Vinegar - 1 Tbls
  • EVOO - 2 tspn
  • Kosher Salt - 1⁄4 tspn
  • Ground Black Pepper - 1⁄4 tspn

Directions:
1. Slice and toast bread using 1 Tbls of EVOO. The easiest way to do this is to toss the bread in a bowl with the oil. It's not imperitive that all of the bread is covered in oil, you just want the oil as spread out as possible. Toast bread until outer edges are a light golden brown around the edges.
2. Toss ingredients together in large bowl: Tomatoes, Basil, Garlic, and Parmesean Cheese. Try not to bruise the tomatoes while mixing ingredients. Then mix in balsalmic vinegar, EVOO, salt, and pepper.
3. Let mixture set, chilled, for at least 1 hour.
4. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread slices.
5.Optional: Fresh Mozzarella Cheese: Slice fresh Mozzarella Cheese very thin and place on top of mixture with fresh basil garnish.
